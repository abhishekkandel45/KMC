About Our Club

Coding the Future

The Computer Club at KMC is a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts, aspiring developers, and digital innovators. We bring together students passionate about technology and provide them with opportunities to learn, create, and excel in the digital world.

From beginner-friendly coding workshops to advanced AI projects, our club offers a comprehensive learning environment where students can develop both technical skills and creative problem-solving abilities.
