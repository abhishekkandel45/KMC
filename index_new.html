About Us

Introducing KMC Student Committee

Students' welfare is our welfare

The KMC Students Committee serves as the paradigmatic body for all students studying at Kathmandu Model Secondary School. Our mission is to stimulate participation in extra-curricular activities, reinforce expertise and craftsmanship, and enable all-embracing development of students.

We act as a bridge between college administration and students, providing platforms to showcase aptitude and achieve greater success. Our committee offers opportunities for both intra and inter-college competitions.
