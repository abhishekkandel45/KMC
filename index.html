Introducing KMC Student Committee

This section will contain the indtroduction of Student committee

The KMC Students Committee serves as the paradigmatic body for all the students studying in the Kathmandu Model Secondary School. The key aim of establishment of the committee is solely focused on stimulating the participation of our fellow mates in extra-curricular activities, to reinforce their expertise and craftsmanship and to enable all-embracing development of the students. The committee acts as a bridge between the college administration and the students in many proportions in which the students can showcase their aptitude and can achieve greater success. The committee provides tenets for such enthusiasts to partake in many intra as well as inter-college competitions.

We have been categorized into 12 clubs namely Art and culture club, Computer club, Dance club, Event Management club, Legal club, Literature club, Media club, Music club, Research and Presentation club, Robotics club, Speech and debate club and social club and an Editor team. The corresponding clubs enable all-round development of the students of the college in their respective realm by organizing various skill-broadening events and sessions. The committee has also been organizing events such as KMC Fun Fest and Techno Fest providing recreation to the students as well in addition to providing the groundwork to showcase their talents.

The committee remains staunched to providing such platforms and groundwork and representing the students of the college through the amalgamation dedication, devotion and diligence.
